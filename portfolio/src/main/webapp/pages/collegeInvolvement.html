I will be a peer guide for the Fall 2021 semester for our Intro to University Life course for incoming freshmen. I am so excited to meet the new class of students, and be there to support them through this program!

These are just a couple of the things that I do on campus, outside of my computer science courses. I can't wait to grow through both my involvements on campus, and my experiences through my classes, during my last two years at TCU!
